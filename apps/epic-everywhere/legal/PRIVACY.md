## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 You provide

- Identity and contact data (name, email, social handle, phone if you share it)  
- Role and request details (e.g., student/parent/founder; “job to be done”)  
- Content you submit in chat, email, forms, Slack, or sessions  
- Payment-related details as needed to match Orders (we do **not** store full card numbers; Stripe processes cards)  
- Feedback, reviews, support tickets  

### 2.2 Collected automatically

- Device and log data (IP address, browser type, approximate location from IP, pages viewed, timestamps)  
- Cookies or local storage for basic site function and analytics if enabled  
- Referral URLs  

### 2.3 From processors / partners

- **Stripe:** payment status, last4, billing country, customer id (per Stripe)  
- **Hosting / Vercel / GitHub:** deployment and repository metadata if you interact with public repos  
- **Communications tools** (e.g., Slack, email providers): message metadata  
- **AI model providers:** prompts/outputs processed to deliver Services (see below)  

### 2.4 Sensitive data

Please **do not** send special-category data (health, precise biometrics, government IDs, full student education records, etc.) unless we explicitly request it for a scoped engagement with appropriate safeguards. For school pilots, we will use a data handling addendum where required.

## 3. How we use information

We use personal information to:

- provide, operate, and improve the Services;  
- process payments and prevent fraud;  
- communicate about Orders, sessions, and support;  
- personalize guidance (role kits, progress notes in our project memory systems);  
- comply with law and enforce Terms;  
- with consent or as otherwise permitted, send product updates (you may opt out of marketing).  

**Legal bases (EEA/UK where applicable):** contract performance; legitimate interests (security, improvement, limited marketing); consent (where required); legal obligation.

---

## 4. AI processing

To deliver coaching, tutoring support, workflows, and drafts, we may send prompts and relevant context to **third-party AI providers** (for example, providers reachable via our infrastructure such as Groq, OpenAI-compatible endpoints, xAI, or others we configure).  

- Do not submit secrets, passwords, or unnecessary personal data of third parties.  
- AI providers process data under their terms; we configure what we can to limit retention where the product allows.  
- AI Output may be stored in session logs or project memory to continue your engagement.

## 7. Retention

We retain information as long as needed for the purposes above:

- Order and tax records: typically **7 years** or as required by law;  
- Support chats and project memory notes: for the relationship plus a reasonable period (often up to **24 months** of inactivity) unless you request deletion sooner and we are not required to keep them;  
- Marketing: until opt-out.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures (access control, secret managers, least-privilege API keys, no secrets in public git). No method of transmission is 100% secure.

## 9. Children

Our marketing site is general audience. **We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in certain regions) for accounts without parental consent.**  

Paid **1:1 video tutoring** involving minors requires a parent/guardian as the contracting party and consent as described at booking. Parents: contact epictechai@gmail.com to review or delete a child’s data we hold.
