# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Epic Tech AI — Epic Everywhere / Epic Desk**  
**Effective date:** July 13, 2026  

This AUP applies to all use of our website, agents, chat, phone, video, and packages. Violations may result in suspension or termination under the Terms of Service.

---

## 1. You may not use the Services to

1. **Break the law** — including fraud, identity theft, harassment, stalking, or threats.  
2. **Exploit children** — any sexual content involving minors; grooming; or circumvention of parental consent rules.  
3. **Cheat on graded work dishonestly** — we help students *learn with AI*; we will not complete exams, proctored assessments, or graded submissions on a student’s behalf in a way that violates academic integrity policies.  
4. **Provide regulated professional advice as if licensed** — do not use our AI Output as a substitute for licensed legal, medical, tax, or financial advice for others.  
5. **Attack systems** — malware, phishing kits, unauthorized access, credential stuffing, or scanning third-party systems without authorization.  
6. **Abuse infrastructure** — excessive automated scraping, spam, or load that degrades the Services.  
7. **Infringe IP** — upload content you do not have rights to use.  
8. **Bypass payment or security** — stolen cards, leaked API keys, or shared accounts meant for a single Customer.  
9. **Generate prohibited content** — including content that promotes violent crimes or severe harm, or that platforms we use forbid.  
10. **Misrepresent affiliation** — claim you are Epic Tech AI or that we endorse your product without permission.

---

## 2. Fair use for text CS and credits

Micro text CS and credits are for **reasonable human-paced** assistance, not for reselling unlimited white-label support or running a call center on our stack. We may rate-limit or re-price abusive patterns.

---

## 3. Sessions (phone / video)

- Be on time; record only with mutual consent.  
- No sharing session links publicly.  
- No sexual content with minors; no harassment of staff or agents.  
- School sessions: follow school policies and consent requirements.

---

Report abuse: **epictechai@gmail.com** with subject “AUP Report.”

## 5. Changes

We may update this AUP with the Terms. Continued use means acceptance.
