---

## 1. Guiding principle

We want you to get value. Digital services and live time are hard to “return,” so refunds depend on **product type** and **whether work or session time was delivered**.

---

## 2. Micro-transactions (generally under USD $50)

| Product type | Refund rule |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Unused credit packs** | Refund within **fourteen (14) days** of purchase if **no credits have been consumed**. Once any credit is used, the pack is non-refundable except as required by law. |
| **Text CS burst / starter pack / workflow fix** | If we have **not started** delivery within **seventy-two (72) hours** of your complete intake message (paid + contact + job description), you may request a full refund. After we begin delivery (first substantive reply with guidance or deliverable), **no refund**, but we will work in good faith to complete the stated scope. |
| **Entry / human tickets** (community or event-style) | **Non-refundable** once issued, except if the event is cancelled by us. |
| **Duplicate / accidental charge** | Full refund if reported within **seven (7) days** with proof. |

---

## 3. Live sessions (phone / video) — when offered

| Situation | Refund |
|-----------|--------|
| You cancel **≥ 24 hours** before scheduled start | Full refund or free reschedule (your choice) |
| You cancel **< 24 hours** or no-show | **No refund**; one reschedule may be offered at our discretion |
| **We** cancel or technical failure on our side | Full refund or free reschedule |
| Session completed | **No refund** |
| Channel not yet live when you paid (pre-sale / waitlist) | Full refund on request **or** credit until channel launches (your choice) |

---

## 4. Packages (e.g., Pilot Landing, Agent Ops Setup, School Pilot)

| Milestone | Refund |
|-----------|--------|
| Paid but **kickoff not started** (no repo/work begun) within **seven (7) days** of us receiving required assets/access | Full refund on request |
| After kickoff / first Deliverable draft shared | Refunds are **discretionary** and typically **pro-rated** only for undelivered fixed milestones we cancel |
| Final Deliverable accepted or used in production by you | **No refund** |
| You fail to provide required materials for **fourteen (14) days** after request | We may cancel and refund **unused** portion only, or close Order as complete if work was blocked by you (case-by-case) |

Change orders and expanded scope are new billable work, not refunds of the original package.

---

## 5. How to request a refund

Email **epictechai@gmail.com** with:

1. Order / Stripe receipt id or payment email  
2. Product SKU  
3. Reason  
4. Whether you prefer refund or credit  

We aim to respond within **three (3) business days**. Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method via Stripe within **five to ten (5–10) business days** after approval (card networks may take longer to show the credit).

---

## 6. Chargebacks

Please contact us before opening a dispute. Friendly fraud chargebacks may lead to suspension of Services. We will provide Stripe with delivery evidence where appropriate.

Nothing in this policy limits **non-waivable** rights under the law of your place of residence (including certain EU/UK cooling-off rights for distance contracts, where they apply and where an exception for digital content does not apply). If such a right applies, we will honor it.
