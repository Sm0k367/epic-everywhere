# Cookie Policy

**Epic Tech AI**  
**Effective date:** July 13, 2026  

---

## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We also use similar technologies such as local storage.

---

## 2. How we use them

| Type | Purpose | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Strictly necessary** | Security, load balancing, basic site operation | Session or short-term |
| **Preferences** | Remember UI choices if implemented | Up to 1 year |
| **Analytics** (if enabled) | Understand traffic and improve the site | Up to 2 years (provider-dependent) |
| **Marketing** | Not used by default on the Epic Everywhere landing | N/A unless disclosed later |

Payment flows on **Stripe** set cookies controlled by Stripe.

---

## 3. Your choices

You can block or delete cookies in browser settings. Strictly necessary cookies may be required for the site to function. Where required by law, we will request consent for non-essential cookies.
